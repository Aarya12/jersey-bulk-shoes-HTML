Our Mission

Jersey Bulk Shoes: Footwear with a Conscience

We're a New Jersey family business with a passion for quality footwear and doing things right. That means our shoes are ethically made in sustainable factories, supporting fair wages and protecting the environment.

Why Choose Sustainable?

Typical shoemaking harms the planet and can exploit workers. Sustainable footwear means recycled materials, less waste, and good conditions for the people who make your shoes. It's a choice you can feel good about!.

We think you'll love our shoes and what we stand for. Explore our collection and choose footwear that makes a difference.

The Jersey Bulk Shoes Difference:

  • Clean, sustainable manufacturing
  • Fair labor practices – everyone deserves a living wage
  • Packaged & shipped from right here in New Jersey
  • Style and sustainability – no compromises!
